Bazaar overview

Click on the item type to show the tables.
Click on an icon to refresh the bazaar and itemmarket prices of the object (1 API call).
Click on the refresh buttons on the top right section title to refresh the items information and bazaar prices (1 API call/item). This feature is not available for the full list.
Each tables contains:

  • Row 1: item icon / click to refresh button
  • Row 2: time since last update / click to delete bazaar data (because why not?)
  • Row 3: item name / click to see its description
  • Row 4: personal stock of the item / keep the mouse hover to see the repartition between inventory, bazaar and display / click to add to your custom list
  • Row 5: current market price / link to torn market for this item
  • Row 6: market price tendencies for a day computed over a week (left) and over a month (right) / click for details
  • The colored rows are information of the players bazaars. Each row corresponds to a bazaar
    • Column 1: the number of item to sell. Quantities starting by a star correspond to concatenated item market sells
    • Column 2: the selling price of one item (if you let the cursor hover it, you can see the cummulative price)
  • The colors correspond to the prices compared to the current market price
    < 3% < 1% ± 1% > 1% > 3%
